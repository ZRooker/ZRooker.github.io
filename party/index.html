The Bridesmaids

Lindsay Millis

Lauren met Lindsay when she was born (But started talking to her months before). Lauren doesn’t remember much about that day, but she thinks she remembers receiving an awesome wooden play kitchen set as a gift from her grandparent in exchange for becoming an older sister. Little did she know, the person who entered the world that day would be stealing that set from her for years to come. Anyone who knows the sisters knows that they loved each other just as much as they fought when they were little, which was a lot (Ask them about the holes they put in the wall). As they got older, the fighting stopped, and they became the best of friends. Though they still don’t agree on everything, the two are inseparable and always have the other’s back. They have talked about their wedding days since they were little girls, and Lauren feels so blessed to have such an amazing sister to act as her Maid of Honor.

Lindsay is currently a Sophomore at Oklahoma State University studying Pre-Nursing. She has applied to be a part of the first class of students in the nursing program at OSU. Lindsay and her boyfriend of 3 years, Jayden, love to build Lego sets together. Lindsay also loves to read and crochet, which she discusses with Lauren frequently. She also spends lots of time looking at Bernese Mountain dog puppies and planning for the one she will have later in life.



Hailey Rooker

Lauren met Hailey during the summer of 2021 after Zarek introduced her to his family. Having seen many shows and movies, Lauren was nervous to meet his sister. Hailey, however, was incredibly kind and welcoming from the first time they met. Knowing that she had to put up with Zarek through all of their lives, Lauren is also impressed that she was able to keep him and his brothers in check. More recently, the two talk often about crafting and their current projects. Every time Lauren spends time with Hailey, it feels like hanging out with a sister, and she is so excited to become Hailey’s (and also to have someone else to ask about family plans to double check the information from Zarek). Though they haven’t known each other for long, Lauren is so thankful for the friendship they have built, and didn’t hesitate to ask Hailey to be a bridesmaid.

Hailey currently works as a second-grade teacher at the elementary school where her mom is now a counselor. This is her first year teaching, but she took over the classroom her mom taught in before her. She is a loving dog parent, with her boyfriend Tsegaye, to their two dachshunds, London and Stark. She also loves to make friendship bracelets and is learning to crochet in her free time.



Taylor Winfrey

Lauren met Taylor the summer before their Freshman year after they were randomly assigned to be roommates. Knowing how randomly assigned roommate situations usually go, Lauren was apprehensive. However, they hit it off immediately and she instantly knew they would be friends. Though their time as roommates was cut short (thanks COVID), they spent more time together than they did with anyone else that year and became the best of friends. Taylor not only put up with Lauren’s incessant singing at all hours of the day, lights on while Lauren studied and she slept, and the literal fire-hazard that Lauren turned her side of the room into, but she also never complained about any of it, willingly joined in any shenanigans Lauren suggested, and made Lauren laugh more than anyone she had ever met. They were the roommate story that the college LOVED to talk about because they became best friends. Though they spent less time together after COVID and lived separate places, Taylor continues to make Lauren laugh every time they speak, and Lauren can’t imagine life without her! Lauren knew after a very short time being roommates that Taylor would be standing with her on her wedding day.

Taylor is currently pursuing her masters degree at Oklahoma State University, and plans to become a speech pathologist. When Taylor has the rare time to spare, she loves to read. She is also a very talented cook and baker.



Sloane Johnson

Lauren met Sloane in a rather unconventional way. When Lauren’s dad, Lance, was leading orientation sessions for incoming engineering freshmen, and Sloane was one of those students. As he talked with her and her mother, Lance decided that she would be an awesome friend for his own incoming engineering freshman, Lauren. The two did get in contact, and Sloane became Lauren’s first college friend. They were not only both engineering students, they also shared many things in common, and were members of several of the same organizations. They spent lots of time together and became better friends during Freshman and Sophomore year, then Sloane asked Lauren to join a group of girls with her that were all renting a house together Junior year. They lived together from the summer of 2021 until they graduated in 2023. Since they met, Sloane has been one of Lauren’s biggest supporters and has always made her feel so loved and appreciated. Lauren knew that support would continue when she asked Sloane to be her bridesmaid.

Sloane currently works as a Civil Engineer at an engineering consulting firm called Langan in Austin Texas. Sloane and her boyfriend, Austin, love to travel, and recently went to Germany together. In her free time, Sloane mentors high school aged girls in a young life group. She also spends lots of time learning to cook impressive meals.



Mika Lucas - Matron of Honor

Lauren met Mika when Mika was in college and began babysitting Lauren and her sister. The three of them spent countless hours together over the span of a several years, driving them to and from school, taking them to tennis and swim lessons, and entertaining them in many creative and fun ways. After she graduated, Mika married Kyle Lucas and they both began working at Stillwater Public Schools, where both girls eventually attended, and they remained close friends of the Millis family. When the Lucas family welcomed their children into the world, they invited the Millis family into the lives of their kids as well. The girls began to babysit their three kids and have continued to do so since their oldest was born. Through the years, the Lucas family has become a part of Lauren’s family. Lauren and her sister both think of Mika as their sister. Lauren could not imagine getting married without Mika at her side.

Mika currently works at Westwood Elementary School as a Counselor. She is an amazing mom to her three kids, Collins, Brooks, and Quinn.
