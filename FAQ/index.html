Frequently Asked Questions

The Dress Code

Men: Slacks or khakis with a dress shirt or a casual polo shirt. A tie is optional, and a jacket may not be necessary.

Women: Sundresses, skirts, or dressy pants with a blouse. Avoid overly formal or extravagant outfits.

What Will Be on the Menu?

The menu is still under consideration. If you have any dietary preferences or special accommodations required, kindly indicate them in the comment section of your RSVP form. Your input will help us ensure a delightful dining experience for all.

Can We Take Photo's During The Ceromony?

We kindly request that you refrain from taking photos during the ceremony. We have enlisted the services of an exceptional photographer and videographer to capture this special moment. Following the event, we will gladly share any desired photographs.

How Do I RSVP?

You can RSVP online HERE. If there is problems with the RSVP or things have changed fill free to submit another RSVP form.

Can I Bring A Plus One?

To ensure that we stay within the venue's guest limit, we kindly request that you exercise discretion when considering a plus one. If you wish to bring a guest not originally included in the invitation, please don't hesitate to reach out and inquire. Your understanding and cooperation are greatly appreciated!
